contract resources and permanent recruitment

Connect delivers great contract resources quickly for projects such as engineers, project manegers, site supervisors, drafters ect, with an extensive network developed since 1994. Having placed many people in multiple projects, we have detailed knowledge of their capabilities

On the permanent rectruitment side, we help with a very systematic process targeted at skill, behavioural and cultural fit (its rigour reduces the chance
of a poor hirling decision), and we search for repeathing patterns of candidate behaviour in their career ( a strong predictor of future behaviour). In
particular, we are skilled in sourcing people who do not respond to advertising, but rather have to be proactively approached.

We work closely with a select range of multidisciplinary engineering and project managerment consultancies. They cap help with discrete packages fo work taht have defined deliverables. (.e.g. a specific design component or project management of a key area).
