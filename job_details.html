business development

  • 26/11/2020
  • Western NSW
  • Permanent
  • $100,000 - $119,999
about the job

We are recruiting for a multi-national firm providing high end manufactured products and engineering solutions globally. Their products and services are typically focused on
pumping and hydraulic processes, tools and machine and plant actuating and automated process systems.

An opportunity has arisen for an experience Business Development Professional to lead the Hydraulic Technologies division.

This role will involve team leading, generating a pipeline for sales opportunities while achieving commercially favourable contract terms and profit margins.

main responsibilities:
  • Implementing business plans relevant to the portfolio
  • Directing the Hydraulic Technologies team
  • Liaising with leaders, key accounts and sales managers to determine sales and delivery processes and develop sales strategies
  • Generating sales leads through different activities
  • Leading targeted market research
  • Execution of sales plans and budgets
  • Business development
  • Maintaining close customer relationship
  • Working together with technical teams to develop proposale and specifications
  • Creating after-sales strategies
  • Reporting on and tracking sales performance
  • Presenting to senior leaders
experience and qualifications:
  • Degree in mechanical/chemical engineering or similar relevant qualification
  • 3 years experience in application engineering
  • Strong knowledge of hydraulic equipment and industrail tools and the Australian hydraulics market
  • 5 years experience in similar sales/BD role

This package includes car allowance and bonus, as well as a competitive salary. The ideal candidate will be based in NSW or VIC with potential flexibility.

If you are interested in this role and have the relevant experience, don't hesitate to apply.
